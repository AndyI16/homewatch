AVR Netino is an extension to Arduino IDE 0.22/0.23 and 1.00 to enable the use of the Arduino IDE with the Pollin AVR NetIO board.

[http://code.google.com/p/avr-netino/]

When switching from Uno to Avr-Netino Atmega32 or Atmega644 there will be several compiler errors to be fixed.

Atmega644: The EWEE definition has to be altered (see avr dir)
